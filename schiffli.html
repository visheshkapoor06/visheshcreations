Schiffli Embroidery

Our State-of-the-Art Swiss Made Saurer Schiffli embroidery machines produce the best quality fabrics and a wide variety of products.
Please follow us on Instagram to see all our latest designs & our complete designs catalogue.

Schiffli Embroidery is an intricate embroidery where designs are sewn onto a base fabric using the schiffli embroidery machine. The schiffli machine is a variation on the sewing machine. It embroiders with a front thread and a bobbin thread which form together a lock stitch, similar to that of a sewing machine.
