Net & Tulle

Our State-of-the-Art German Made Karl Mayer Warp Knitting machines produce the best quality fabrics and a wide variety of products.
Please follow us on Instagram to see all our latest designs & our complete designs catalogue.

Net & Tulle is a lightweight mesh fabric that has a sheer netlike or patterned structure. It is easy to dye and comes in multiple color variations & patterns. This delicate and gaudy fabric is used for finishing and separate garments. Tulle fabrics are a perfect pick for evening dresses and wedding attire.
