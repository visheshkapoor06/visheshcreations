Guipure Fabrics

Our State-of-the-Art Swiss Made Saurer Schiffli embroidery machines produce the best quality fabrics and a wide variety of products.
Please follow us on Instagram to see all our latest designs & our complete designs catalogue.

Guipure Fabrics also known as GPO Fabrics are made on schiffli machines. Guipure lace fabric is a delicate type of lace created of convex lace elements which are connected with bars or plaits, resulting in an airy ornament. Its structure makes it one of the most elegant options for cocktail, evening and bridal dresses, blouses or accessories. In fashion context, guipure is widely perceived as a regal fabric imbuing women’s textured outfits with a particular grace.
