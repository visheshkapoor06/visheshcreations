Raschel & Jacquard Fabric

Our State-of-the-Art German Made Karl Mayer Warp Knitting machines produce the best quality fabrics and a wide variety of products.
Please follow us on Instagram to see all our latest designs & our complete designs catalogue.

Raschel & Jacquard fabric is a type of cloth featuring an intricate pattern woven into the warp on a special mechanical loom, The fabric has an openwork pattern and three-dimensional surface effect. These fabrics are available in a variety of compositions and weights and serve various purposes.
