Schiffli Embroidery

Our State-of-the-Art Swiss Made Saurer Schiffli embroidery machines produce the best quality fabrics and a wide variety of products.

Schiffli Embroidery

Schiffli embroidery is done on a vast variety of fabrics such as Cambric, Voil, Poplin, Flex, Denim, Chambray, Organza, Hosiery, etc using Cotton yarns, Dyed Polyester yarns, Lurex yarns, etc.

Guipure Laces

Guipure Laces also known as Chemical Laces are made on schiffli machines. These laces consist of embroidered motifs held together by large stitches.

Guipure Fabrics

Guipue Fabrics also known as Chemical Fabrics are made on schiffli machines. These are same as Guipure laces but are made as allover embroideries and used in fabric form.

Embroidery on Net/Tulle

Schiffli Embroidery done on Net/Tulle fabrics. It is done on high quality Tulle manufactured in-house.

Appliqué Embroidery

The addition of a second fabric during the embroidery process is called Appliqué embroidery. This technique requires a double embroidery process to give applique work its unique look and feel.

3D Embroidery

3D Embroidery also involves a double embroidery process giving the finished fabric a unique 3D looks with motifs hanging from the fabric.

Net/Tulle

Net/Tulle Fabrics are manufactured using in-house warp knitting machines. Various types of meshes are made such as plain, dot-nets, star-nets, etc are made in Cotton, Nylon & Polyester.

Raschel & Jacquard Fabrics

Raschel & Jacquard Fabrics also known as Lace Fabrics are manufactured in-house using Warp knitting machines using Cotton, Nylon & Polyester yarns.

Neck Motifs

Neck Motifs are made using Cotton & Polyester yarns used in garments as an embelishment.
