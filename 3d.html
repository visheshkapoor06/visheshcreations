3D Embroidery

Our State-of-the-Art Swiss Made Saurer Schiffli embroidery machines produce the best quality fabrics and a wide variety of products.
Please follow us on Instagram to see all our latest designs & our complete designs catalogue.

3D Embroidery involves a double embroidery process giving the finished fabric a unique 3D look with motifs hanging from the fabric. It involves the technique used for guipure laces to create the 3D motifs and the finesse of delicate schiffli embroidery creating the most elegant and premium fabric used mostly in High fashion and bridal dresses.
